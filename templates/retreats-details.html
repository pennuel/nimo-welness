The RenewHER Essence Wellness Retreat is a carefully curated transformative experience designed for women over 35, focusing on deep healing, personal growth, and reconnection with their most authentic selves. Set against the breathtaking backdrop of the Berkshires, Massachusetts, in the serene embrace of autumn, this retreat offers a sanctuary for women to pause, reflect, and embark on a transformative journey of self-discovery.

Retreat Overview

Nights

3

Rooms

Private/ Shared

Meals

Nutritious meals accommodating any dietary restrictions.

What's Included

The RenewHER Essence spans over a long weekend, designed to immerse participants in a series of carefully planned activities including:

  • Guided Meditation + Yoga
  • Nature Walks
  • Art Therapy + Journaling Workshops
  • Group Discussions + Sharing Circles
  • Wellness Workshops
  • Cultural + Spiritual Sessions

What's Not to Expect

We're thrilled to have you join us, but please note that the following are not included in your retreat package:

  • Transportation: You’ll need to arrange your own travel to and from the retreat.
  • Personal Expenses: Any extra purchases, like souvenirs, are on you.
  • Optional Activities: Activities outside our planned schedule aren’t included.
  • Travel Insurance: We recommend securing your own travel insurance.
  • Private Sessions: Individual wellness treatments will have an additional cost.
