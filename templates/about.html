We Transform Lives Together

The Journey of Wellness and Healing is a journey of rediscovery, a journey of transformation, a journey of self-love and self-care.

At Nimo Wellness, we believe in the power of holistic wellness to transform lives. Our approach is rooted in the philosophy that every individual is unique and requires personalized care.

Our Mission

At Nimo, We want to help you rebuild your physical, mental, emotional, and social well-being.

Our focus is to help you discover your inner Radiance and address the root cause of your health concerns and challenges.

Through our Mind, Body, Spirit focused Retreats,
We help you Reset, Refocus, Re-adjust, and Restart the Inner you empowering you to live a life of vitality, joy, and purpose.

Meet the Host

Hello, I'm Nimo, founder of Nimo Wellness. My journey, rooted in Kenya's vibrant landscapes, has woven a tapestry of cultures, adventures, and personal quests for healing. Traveling to over 35 countries fueled my curiosity and growth, yet amidst exploration, I faced postpartum depression, igniting my purpose. Drawing on my nursing background and wellness coaching, I embraced holistic wellness, realizing it as my calling.
